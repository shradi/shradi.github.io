Five Nights at Yiker's

Spend five anti-frightful nights at Yiker's, a fan-game based on the FNaF series by Scott Cawthon. Now featuring emergency light-deinstallation and a multitude of stars! Defend yourself from thieves by using your cameras, lights, and your doors. Cutscenes and phone recordings will tell you the story that's unfolding at Yiker's Fun Time World of... uh, I forgot. Anyway, have fun! Report any bugs you find to whoever you think will be able to fix them ;)
