  • Five Nights at Yikers

    • Spend five anti-frightful nights at Yiker's, a fan-game based on the FNaF series by Scott Cawthon. Now featuring emergency light-deinstallation and a multitude of stars! Defend yourself from thieves by using your cameras, lights, and your doors. Cutscenes and phone recordings will tell you the story that's unfolding at Yiker's Fun Time World of... uh, I forgot. Anyway, have fun! Report any bugs you find to whoever you think will be able to fix them ;)

  • Harold's Fishing Trip

    • Harold decided to go fishing one day. After being dragged underwater by a large fish, he sees now that it was a bad decision. Press space to advance through cutscenes. Use mouse to move around, unless you turned on arrow controls on the main menu. There's a secret hard mode, but only good friends will know it ;)

  • Stiick Studio

    • Create a neat stick figure called a Stiick with this silly little thingy. Just click around, and you can copy your Stiick code above, and then share it with friends by inputting it in the corner. There are a few easter eggs. Can ya find them?

  • Snake 3: Otter Above All

    • Control the late Rosa the sea otter as she dodges the scary boneworms! This game was created for a charity livestream by DougDoug, it's filled to the brim with inside jokes, don't expect to get them all.
